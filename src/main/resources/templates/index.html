How to Play

Welcome to the Detective and Suspect game! Here are the rules and objectives:

  • The game is played between a Detective and a Suspect.
  • The goal of the Detective is to catch the Suspect by moving to the same position on the map.
  • The Suspect wins if they remain uncaught after 30 rounds.
  • Detectives and the Suspect can move around the map using either a taxi or a train.
  • The Suspect has unlimited tickets for travel, but the Detectives have a limited number of tickets.
  • If the Detectives run out of tickets before catching the Suspect, they lose the game.
  • After every 6 rounds, the Suspect's position will be revealed to the Detectives.
